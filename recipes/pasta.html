pasta recipe

Spaghetti meatballs that have been inspired by The Castle of Cagliostro. it's easy to make and delicious.

ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • tomato pasta sauce
  • sausages (for the meatballs)
  • parmesan cheese

steps

  1. cook the pasta as per directions
  2. heat the pasta sauce
  3. squeeze the meat out of the sausages into the shape of meat balls
  4. fry the meat balls
  5. add the pasta, pasta sauce, and meatballs together
  6. serve onto plates and add cheese
